8) COLOR PALETTE

9) RESPONSIVE BREAKPOINTS
-------------------------
- >= 1025px: 4 columns
- 781px–1024px: 3 columns
- 521px–780px: 2 columns
- <= 520px: 1 column


10) INTERACTION STATES
----------------------
- Category chip default: white background, gray border
- Category chip active: indigo background, white text
- Category chip hover: subtle translateY(-1px), border highlight
- Card hover: translateY(-4px), deeper shadow
- Button hover: translateY(-1px)
- Inputs focus: visible indigo focus ring
